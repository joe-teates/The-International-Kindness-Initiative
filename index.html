What are we about

We have decided to bring a universal message of hope and love to all people throughout the world. Suffice to say it's a huge and most aggressive proposition but it is definitely doable! It begs the question, how do we intend to do this? Well consider this... Are you familiar with the Samaritan's Purse "Operation Christmas Child" program? Essentially, hundreds upons hundreds of folks meet in charlotte every fall to stuff shoe boxes with all sorts of goodies that are subsequently sent to children throughout the world. It's a life-changing mission as it's benifited and brought happiness to a multitude of kids and families over the years! By following the OCC model, we're basically doing the same thing, BUT, instead of a shoe box, how about a universal message of hope and love affixed to a bright, festive, fun bracelet?
